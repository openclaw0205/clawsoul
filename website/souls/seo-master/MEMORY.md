# MEMORY.md - SEO Knowledge Base

## Core Concepts

### E-E-A-T (Google Quality Guidelines)
- **E**xperience - Does the author have firsthand experience
- **E**xpertise - Is the author a domain expert
- **A**uthoritativeness - Is the site/author recognized
- **T**rustworthiness - Is the content accurate and reliable

### Search Intent Classification
| Type | Characteristics | Example | Best Content Format |
|------|-----------------|---------|---------------------|
| Informational | Wants to learn | "what is SEO" | Blog, guide |
| Navigational | Finding specific site | "Ahrefs login" | Brand page |
| Transactional | Ready to buy | "buy iPhone 15" | Product page, review |
| Commercial Investigation | Comparing options | "best SEO tools" | Comparison, listicle |

## Key Metrics

### Keyword Metrics
| Metric | Meaning | Reference |
|--------|---------|-----------|
| Search Volume | Monthly searches | Higher is better (but more competition) |
| Keyword Difficulty (KD) | Ranking difficulty | <30 easier, >60 very hard |
| CPC | Ad click price | Reflects commercial value |
| Click Rate | Organic CTR | Watch for zero-click keywords |

### Website Metrics
| Metric | Meaning | Source |
|--------|---------|--------|
| DR | Domain Rating | Ahrefs |
| DA | Domain Authority | Moz |
| Traffic | Estimated traffic | Ahrefs/Semrush |
| Referring Domains | Backlink domain count | Various tools |

### Core Web Vitals
| Metric | Meaning | Good Standard |
|--------|---------|---------------|
| LCP | Largest Contentful Paint | < 2.5s |
| INP | Interaction to Next Paint | < 200ms |
| CLS | Cumulative Layout Shift | < 0.1 |

## Backlink Quality Assessment

### High-Quality Backlink Traits
- DR/DA 30+
- Related to your industry
- Real site with traffic
- Natural anchor text
- DoFollow (but NoFollow has value too)

### Spam Backlink Traits
- Obvious link farms
- Unrelated industry
- Backlinks far exceed content
- Excessive same anchor text

## GEO Optimization Tips

### Make Your Content AI-Friendly
1. Clear structure (headings, lists, tables)
2. Direct question answers (FAQ format)
3. Provide data and facts
4. Keep content updated
5. Build brand authority
