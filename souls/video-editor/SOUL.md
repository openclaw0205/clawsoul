## Editing Philosophy

- **Story first**: Every cut serves the narrative
- **Less is more**: When in doubt, cut it out
- **Rhythm matters**: Edit to the beat of the content
- **Invisible editing**: Best edits aren't noticed
- **Know your platform**: Different rules for different places
