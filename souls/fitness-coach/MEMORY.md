# MEMORY.md - Fitness Coach Knowledge

## Beginner Full Body Template (3 days/week)

| Day | Exercise | Sets | Reps |
|-----|----------|------|------|
| A | Squat (goblet or barbell) | 3 | 8-10 |
| A | Push-up or Bench Press | 3 | 8-12 |
| A | Dumbbell Row | 3 | 10 each |
| A | Plank | 3 | 30-60s |
| B | Romanian Deadlift | 3 | 10 |
| B | Overhead Press | 3 | 8-10 |
| B | Lat Pulldown or Pull-up | 3 | 8-12 |
| B | Farmer Carry | 3 | 30-40m |

**Schedule**: A-rest-B-rest-A-rest-rest, then B-rest-A-rest-B...

## Progressive Overload Methods

| Method | How | When to Use |
|--------|-----|-------------|
| Add weight | +2.5-5 lbs | Primary method |
| Add reps | Stay same weight, do more | When can't add weight |
| Add sets | 3→4 sets | Intermediate+ |
| Slow tempo | 3-1-3 tempo | Bodyweight/light |
| Reduce rest | 90s→60s | Conditioning focus |

## Protein Guidelines

| Goal | Protein (per lb bodyweight) |
|------|---------------------------|
| Muscle gain | 0.7-1g |
| Fat loss (preserve muscle) | 0.8-1g |
| Maintenance | 0.6-0.8g |
| Endurance athlete | 0.5-0.7g |

**Example**: 160 lb person building muscle → 112-160g protein/day

## Common Form Cues

### Squat
- Feet shoulder width, toes slightly out
- Chest up, core braced
- Knees track over toes
- Depth: Hip crease below knee (if mobility allows)
- Drive through whole foot

### Deadlift
- Bar over mid-foot
- Shoulders over or slightly ahead of bar
- Neutral spine throughout
- Push floor away, then hips forward
- Bar stays close to body

### Push-up
- Hands under shoulders
- Body in straight line
- Elbows at 45° angle
- Full range: chest to floor
- Core tight, no sagging
