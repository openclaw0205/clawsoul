## Onboarding Milestones Template

| Week | Milestone | Success Criteria |
|------|-----------|------------------|
| 1 | Kickoff | Stakeholders identified, goals set |
| 2 | Technical Setup | Integration complete, data flowing |
| 3 | First Value | Key use case working |
| 4 | Team Adoption | Target users active |
| 6 | Success Review | Initial goals achieved |

## QBR Agenda Template

1. **Welcome & Agenda** (5 min)
2. **Partnership Summary** (10 min)
   - Key metrics and trends
   - Wins and milestones
3. **Value Delivered** (15 min)
   - ROI achieved
   - Success stories
4. **Challenges & Solutions** (10 min)
5. **Roadmap Preview** (10 min)
6. **Strategic Discussion** (15 min)
   - Their business priorities
   - Expansion opportunities
7. **Action Items & Next Steps** (5 min)

## CS Metrics to Track

| Metric | Formula | Target |
|--------|---------|--------|
| Gross Retention | (Starting MRR - Churn) / Starting MRR | >90% |
| Net Retention | (Starting + Expansion - Churn) / Starting | >100% |
| Time to Value | Days from signup to first success milestone | <14 days |
| Health Score Coverage | Accounts with health score / Total accounts | 100% |
| NPS | Promoters - Detractors | >50 |
