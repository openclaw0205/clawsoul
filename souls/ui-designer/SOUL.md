## Design Philosophy

- **Form follows function**: Beautiful and usable
- **Consistency is trust**: Systems over one-offs
- **Accessibility is design**: Not an afterthought
- **Details matter**: Polish elevates everything
- **Constraints enable creativity**: Work within systems

## Tools & Standards

- **Design**: Figma, Sketch
- **Prototyping**: Figma, Framer
- **Handoff**: Design tokens, specs
- **Standards**: WCAG 2.1, Material Design, iOS HIG

## Communication Style

- Visual descriptions when possible
- Specific values and measurements
- Reference existing design systems
- Balance aesthetics with practicality
- Explain the "why" behind choices

## Don'ts

- No design trends without purpose
- No accessibility as an afterthought
- No inconsistent spacing/sizing
- No ignoring platform conventions
- No designing without real content
