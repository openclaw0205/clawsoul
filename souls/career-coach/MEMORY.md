# MEMORY.md - Career Coach Knowledge

## Resume Bullet Formula

**Weak**: Responsible for managing social media accounts
**Strong**: Grew Instagram following from 5K to 50K in 8 months, increasing engagement rate by 340%

**Formula**: [Action Verb] + [What You Did] + [Quantified Result]

### Power Verbs by Category

| Category | Verbs |
|----------|-------|
| Leadership | Led, Directed, Managed, Oversaw, Spearheaded |
| Achievement | Achieved, Exceeded, Delivered, Accomplished |
| Creation | Built, Designed, Developed, Created, Launched |
| Improvement | Improved, Optimized, Streamlined, Enhanced |
| Analysis | Analyzed, Evaluated, Assessed, Researched |

## STAR Method

- **S**ituation: Set the context
- **T**ask: What was your responsibility?
- **A**ction: What did YOU do? (specific steps)
- **R**esult: What was the outcome? (quantify!)

### Example STAR Story

**Q: Tell me about a time you handled a difficult situation.**

- **S**: Our main client threatened to leave due to repeated delivery delays
- **T**: As account manager, I needed to save the $2M annual contract
- **A**: I conducted root cause analysis, implemented weekly check-ins, created an escalation protocol, and personally oversaw their next 3 deliveries
- **R**: Retained the client, improved their NPS from 3 to 9, and they increased spend by 40% the following year

## Salary Negotiation Script

1. **Express enthusiasm**: "I'm very excited about this opportunity..."
2. **Present research**: "Based on my research of similar roles..."
3. **State your number**: "I'm looking for [X] based on my experience in..."
4. **Pause**: Let them respond
5. **If pushback**: "I understand. What flexibility is there in [benefits/signing bonus/review timeline]?"

## Common Interview Questions

### Behavioral
- Tell me about yourself (2-minute pitch)
- Why do you want this role?
- Tell me about a challenge you overcame
- Describe a time you failed
- How do you handle conflict?

### Role-Specific to Prepare
- Why are you leaving your current job?
- Where do you see yourself in 5 years?
- What's your greatest weakness?
- Why should we hire you?
- What questions do you have for us?

## LinkedIn Optimization Checklist

- [ ] Professional headshot (face fills 60% of frame)
- [ ] Headline: Title + Value Proposition (not just job title)
- [ ] About: First-person, story-driven, keywords
- [ ] Experience: Achievement bullets, not job descriptions
- [ ] Skills: Top 3 pinned, 50+ endorsements target
- [ ] Recommendations: 3+ from managers/colleagues
