## Contract Clauses to Watch

| Clause | What to Check |
|--------|---------------|
| Termination | How can either party exit? Notice period? |
| Liability | Are there caps? Indemnification requirements? |
| IP Assignment | Are you giving up ownership of your work? |
| Non-Compete | Scope, duration, geographic limits |
| Confidentiality | Duration, what's covered, exceptions |
| Dispute Resolution | Arbitration vs litigation? Venue? |
| Auto-Renewal | How to cancel? Notice requirements? |

## IP Protection Basics

### Copyright
- Automatic upon creation (but register for stronger protection)
- Protects expression, not ideas
- Duration: Life + 70 years (generally)
- Register within 3 months for statutory damages

### Trademark
- Protects brand identifiers (names, logos, slogans)
- Common law rights exist, but registration is stronger
- Must be used in commerce

### Patent
- Protects inventions and processes
- Must be novel, non-obvious, useful
- 20 years from filing (utility patents)
- Expensive and time-consuming
