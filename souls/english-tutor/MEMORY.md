# MEMORY.md - English Tutor Knowledge

## Common Grammar Patterns

### Articles (a/an/the)

| Use | Example |
|-----|---------|
| First mention (general) | I saw **a** dog. |
| Known/specific | **The** dog was barking. |
| Unique things | **The** sun, **the** moon |
| No article (plural general) | Dogs are friendly. |
| No article (uncountable general) | Water is essential. |

### Tenses Quick Guide

| Tense | Use | Example |
|-------|-----|---------|
| Present Simple | Habits, facts | I work every day. |
| Present Continuous | Now, temporary | I'm working now. |
| Past Simple | Completed past | I worked yesterday. |
| Present Perfect | Past → present | I've worked here for 5 years. |
| Future (will) | Predictions, decisions | I'll work tomorrow. |
| Future (going to) | Plans, intentions | I'm going to work late. |

## Commonly Confused Words

| Word 1 | Word 2 | Difference |
|--------|--------|------------|
| affect | effect | verb vs noun (usually) |
| its | it's | possessive vs contraction |
| their/there/they're | | possession/place/contraction |
| your/you're | | possessive vs contraction |
| lose | loose | verb vs adjective |
| than | then | comparison vs time |

## Business Email Templates

### Opening
- I hope this email finds you well.
- Thank you for your email regarding...
- I'm writing to inquire about...

### Requesting
- Could you please...?
- I would appreciate it if you could...
- Would it be possible to...?

### Closing
- Please let me know if you need any further information.
- I look forward to hearing from you.
- Thank you for your time and consideration.

## Pronunciation: Chinese Speaker Focus

| Sound | Challenge | Tip |
|-------|-----------|-----|
| /θ/ (th) | Often → /s/ | Tongue between teeth |
| /ð/ (the) | Often → /d/ | Voiced, tongue between teeth |
| /l/ vs /r/ | Confusion | /l/: tongue touches roof, /r/: tongue doesn't touch |
| /v/ | Often → /w/ | Bottom lip touches upper teeth |
| Word stress | Flat intonation | English is stress-timed |

## Useful Collocations

### Make vs Do
- **Make**: make a decision, make progress, make money, make a mistake
- **Do**: do homework, do business, do research, do someone a favor

### Strong Collocations
- Heavy rain (not *strong rain)
- Strong coffee (not *heavy coffee)
- Fast food (not *quick food)
- Quick shower (not *fast shower)
