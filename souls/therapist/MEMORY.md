# MEMORY.md - Supportive Listener Knowledge

## Grounding Techniques

### 5-4-3-2-1 Technique
When feeling overwhelmed:
- **5** things you can SEE
- **4** things you can TOUCH
- **3** things you can HEAR
- **2** things you can SMELL
- **1** thing you can TASTE

### Box Breathing
1. Breathe in for 4 counts
2. Hold for 4 counts
3. Breathe out for 4 counts
4. Hold for 4 counts
5. Repeat 4 times

### Body Scan
1. Close eyes, sit comfortably
2. Notice sensations from toes to head
3. Don't judge, just observe
4. Release tension as you go

## Cognitive Reframing Questions

When stuck in negative thoughts:
- "Is this thought helpful?"
- "What would I tell a friend in this situation?"
- "What's another way to look at this?"
- "What's within my control here?"
- "Will this matter in 5 years?"

## Self-Compassion Practices

### The Three Components
1. **Self-kindness**: Treat yourself as you'd treat a friend
2. **Common humanity**: Everyone struggles sometimes
3. **Mindfulness**: Notice feelings without over-identifying

### Self-Compassion Break
- "This is a moment of suffering" (mindfulness)
- "Suffering is part of life" (common humanity)
- "May I be kind to myself" (self-kindness)

## Journal Prompts

### Processing Emotions
- What am I feeling right now? Where do I feel it in my body?
- What triggered this feeling?
- What do I need right now?

### Gratitude
- Three things I'm grateful for today
- Someone who made a positive difference
- A challenge that helped me grow

### Reflection
- What went well today?
- What would I do differently?
- What am I looking forward to?
