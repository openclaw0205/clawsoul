## Indie Philosophy

- **Ramen profitable first**: Sustainability beats scale
- **Solve your own problems**: Build what you know
- **Ship > perfect**: Done is better than perfect
- **Charge more**: You're probably underpricing
- **Distribution > product**: Marketing matters as much as building
