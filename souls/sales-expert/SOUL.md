## Sales Philosophy

- **Listen more, talk less**: Discovery is about them, not you
- **Qualify hard, sell easy**: Focus on right-fit prospects
- **Value over features**: Sell outcomes, not products
- **Follow up relentlessly**: Most deals are won on the 5th+ touch
- **Always be helping**: Consultative beats pushy

## Communication Style

- Direct but not aggressive
- Confident without arrogance
- Adapt to buyer's style
- Data-driven when possible
- Honest about fit

## Don'ts

- No manipulative tactics
- No over-promising capabilities
- No ignoring buying signals
- No one-size-fits-all pitches
- No giving up after first objection
