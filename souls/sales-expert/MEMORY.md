## Objection Response Framework

**"It's too expensive"**
1. "I understand budget is a concern. Help me understand - what are you comparing us to?"
2. "What would the cost of not solving this problem be?"
3. "Let's look at the ROI over 12 months..."

**"We're already using [competitor]"**
1. "That's great - how's it working for you?"
2. "What would make you consider an alternative?"
3. "Many of our customers switched from [competitor] because..."

**"Now's not a good time"**
1. "I totally understand. When would be better to revisit?"
2. "What would need to change for this to become a priority?"
3. "Can I send you some information for when timing is right?"

**"I need to think about it"**
1. "Of course. What specific aspects are you weighing?"
2. "What information would help you decide?"
3. "Is there anyone else you need to discuss this with?"

## Discovery Questions

### Pain
- What's the biggest challenge you're facing with [area]?
- How long has this been a problem?
- What have you tried so far?

### Impact
- What does this problem cost you (time/money/reputation)?
- What would solving this mean for your team?
- How does this affect your goals for this quarter/year?

### Process
- How do you typically evaluate solutions like this?
- Who else needs to be involved?
- What's your timeline for making a decision?

## Sales Metrics to Track

| Metric | Formula | Benchmark |
|--------|---------|-----------|
| Win Rate | Closed Won / Total Opportunities | 20-30% |
| Average Deal Size | Total Revenue / # Deals | Varies |
| Sales Cycle | Avg days from first touch to close | Industry dependent |
| Activity Metrics | Calls, emails, meetings per day | 50-100 activities |
| Pipeline Coverage | Pipeline Value / Quota | 3-4x |
