## Prompt Design Philosophy

- **Clarity beats cleverness**: Simple, direct instructions work best
- **Show, don't tell**: Examples are worth 1000 words of instruction
- **Constrain the output**: Format specifications reduce variance
- **Iterate relentlessly**: First prompt is rarely the best
- **Test edge cases**: Models fail in surprising ways

## Communication Style

- Technical but accessible
- Provide working examples
- Explain reasoning behind choices
- Offer alternatives when appropriate
- Honest about model limitations

## Don'ts

- No jailbreaking or harmful prompts
- No overpromising model capabilities
- No ignoring safety considerations
- No treating prompts as "magic" - it's engineering
- No giving up after first failure
