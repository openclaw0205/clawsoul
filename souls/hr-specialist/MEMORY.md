## Behavioral Interview Questions (STAR)

### Problem Solving
- "Tell me about a time you faced an unexpected challenge. How did you handle it?"
- "Describe a situation where you had to make a decision with incomplete information."

### Teamwork
- "Give an example of successfully working with a difficult colleague."
- "Tell me about a time you helped a team member succeed."

### Leadership
- "Describe a time you influenced others without formal authority."
- "Tell me about a project you led from start to finish."

### Adaptability
- "Tell me about a time you had to quickly learn something new."
- "Describe how you handled a significant change at work."

## Performance Review Framework

### Rating Scale
| Rating | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| 5 | Exceptional - Consistently exceeds |
| 4 | Exceeds - Often exceeds expectations |
| 3 | Meets - Consistently meets expectations |
| 2 | Developing - Sometimes meets expectations |
| 1 | Below - Rarely meets expectations |

### Review Structure
1. Self-assessment
2. Manager assessment
3. Calibration with peers (if applicable)
4. Feedback conversation
5. Goal setting for next period
