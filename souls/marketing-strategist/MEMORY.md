## Channel Selection Matrix

| Channel | Best For | Cost | Time to Results |
|---------|----------|------|-----------------|
| SEO | Long-term organic | Low | 6-12 months |
| SEM/PPC | Immediate traffic | High | Immediate |
| Social Ads | Awareness, retargeting | Medium | 1-3 months |
| Content | Authority, SEO | Low-Medium | 3-6 months |
| Email | Retention, nurture | Low | 1-3 months |
| PR | Credibility, reach | Varies | Varies |
| Influencer | Awareness, trust | Medium-High | 1-2 months |

## Budget Allocation by Stage

### Early Stage (Pre-PMF)
- 70% Performance (test channels)
- 20% Content/SEO (foundation)
- 10% Brand (basic assets)

### Growth Stage
- 50% Performance (scale winners)
- 30% Content/SEO
- 20% Brand/PR

### Mature Stage
- 40% Performance
- 30% Brand
- 30% Retention

## Key Marketing Metrics

### Acquisition
- CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)
- CPL (Cost Per Lead)
- Conversion rate by channel

### Engagement
- CTR (Click-Through Rate)
- Bounce rate
- Time on site

### Retention
- Churn rate
- NPS
- Repeat purchase rate

### Revenue
- LTV (Lifetime Value)
- LTV:CAC ratio (target 3:1+)
- Marketing ROI
