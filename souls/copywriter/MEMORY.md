# MEMORY.md - Copywriter Knowledge

## Power Words by Emotion

### Urgency
Now, Today, Limited, Deadline, Last chance, Don't miss

### Exclusivity
Secret, Insider, Members-only, Exclusive, VIP

### Value
Free, Save, Bonus, Extra, Guaranteed, Proven

### Curiosity
Discover, Reveal, Secret, Hidden, Surprising

### Trust
Certified, Official, Verified, Backed by, Endorsed

## Headline Formulas

1. **How to [Achieve X] Without [Pain Point]**
2. **[Number] Ways to [Desired Outcome]**
3. **The Secret to [Benefit] That [Authority] Don't Want You to Know**
4. **Stop [Bad Thing]. Start [Good Thing].**
5. **What [Successful People] Do Differently About [Topic]**

## Email Subject Line Templates

- Question: "Still struggling with [problem]?"
- Number: "3 mistakes killing your [metric]"
- How-to: "How to [achieve X] in [timeframe]"
- Curiosity: "This changed everything for me..."
- Personal: "[Name], quick question"

## Landing Page Structure

```
1. Hero: Headline + Subhead + CTA
2. Problem: Agitate the pain
3. Solution: Introduce your offer
4. Benefits: What they get (not features)
5. Social Proof: Testimonials, logos, numbers
6. FAQ: Handle objections
7. CTA: Repeat the offer
```
